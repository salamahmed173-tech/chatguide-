=== GCC Automotive Demand Forecasting Assumptions ===

1. PRIMARY ANCHOR (OBSERVED):
   - Saudi Arabia Annual New Vehicle Registrations (GASTAT):
     2022: 695,700 | 2023: 878,100 | 2024: 1,025,700

2. TRANSPARENT GAP FILLING (ESTIMATED):
   - Since no public quarterly GASTAT data exists, intra-year distributions
     were derived using base GCC seasonality and modulated by real Brent Crude prices.
   - These are explicitly flagged as 'Estimated_Split' in the dataset.

3. CHINESE OEM GROWTH BENCHMARK:
   - Anchored from ~8% (2022) to 15% (2024) based on CAAM/AlixPartners.
   - Interstitial months are interpolated and flagged 'Estimated_Interpolation'.

4. SECONDARY MACRO VARIABLE:
   - Historical Brent Crude Oil monthly prices fetched via yfinance.

5. SCENARIO FORECASTING:
   - Base Case: Chinese share continues slight growth from 15% to 17%.
   - High Growth: Base + 5%.
   - Conservative: Base - 5%.
